Hearts of Iron IV


Hearts of Iron IV2014 is a famous World War II simulation game developed by Swedish game publisher Paradox Interactive. It is a work in the "Hearts of Iron" series.

The story background of the game is set around the Second World War, during the anti fascist war. (or maybe not)The default time is 1936-1948. If the war is not over, the battle time can be extended until the war is over. In the game, players can manipulate any country in the world to participate in the epic World War. During the game, many historical events will occur, and some historical events will have a certain probability to develop in the opposite direction, such as the assassination of Trotsky, the Hindenburg and the round the world flight of Amelia Earhart.

In the game, players can see many celebrities in World War II, such as Hitler, Churchill, Stalin and so on. A new and more useful currency was also used. Choosing a good leader and government in the game is very important to the development of the country. Players can even choose different companies to help them improve the production of equipment and materials.
