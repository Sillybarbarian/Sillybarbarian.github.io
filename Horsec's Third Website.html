Ori and the Wisps of Spirits


Ori and the Wisps of Spirits2020 is a Metroidvania game produced by the Moon Studio. The game is famous for its extremely beautiful and exquisite pictures. This work is the sequel of the first generation work "Ori and the Blind Forest". As always, the work retains its excellent picture quality and high game difficulty. In particular, the combat system of the second generation game is richer than that of the first generation. The overall duration of the game process is also twice as long as the previous one.

The game mainly tells about a son of the divine tree, also known as "spirit". While flying in the sky with a friend who has just acquired his flying ability. Unfortunately, they were caught in a storm and fell into another forest ruled by the other divine tree.And finally saved his friend and the whole forest.
